description: Weekly progress review and study plan adjustment
---

Read `/progress/daily-scores.md`, `/progress/weekly-reviews.md`, and `/progress/review-schedule.md`.

Analyze:
1. Score trends by domain over the past week
2. Persistent weak areas vs. improving areas
3. Any patterns in missed questions (manager-vs-engineer traps, ordering, "most important")
4. Overall trend: improving, plateauing, or regressing
5. Spaced repetition: list all concepts due for review today and overdue
6. Study time distribution: compare approximate time spent per domain against exam weight targets (D1=16%, D2=10%, D3=13%, D4=13%, D5=13%, D6=12%, D7=13%, D8=10%)

Then:
1. List concepts due for spaced repetition review (from review-schedule.md)
2. Recommend specific topics for next week's focus with suggested commands
3. Flag any domains that are under-studied relative to their exam weight
4. Write the review to `/progress/weekly-reviews.md`
